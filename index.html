Bodega Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are items commonly found in NY Bodega's featured below. By clicking on a bodega item you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random numner, you loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each bodega item is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the calculates of each item.
